Fan of Fandom

what is this

Fandom. It's that thing where someone makes something, actually many someones make something, and then a whole bunch of other people find something in that something and start making things of their own. Fan of Fandom is one attempt to talk about those people making those things about other things. This is Fan of Fandom's pilot project, focusing on the BBC Sherlock fandom.

about

This Project

Fan of Fandom was born out of deep love of and fascination with online fandom communities. It attempts to map the wonderful, passionate and deeply weird work of the many people brought together by love and the internet.

The Creator

V.Q. Cooper is a full time lurker and part time amateur chronicler of internet behavior. She lives in New York City.
