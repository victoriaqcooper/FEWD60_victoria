This Project

Fan of Fandom was born out of deep love of and fascination with online fandom communities. It attempts to map the wonderful, passionate and deeply weird work of thousands of people brought together by love and the internet.

The Creator

V.Q. Cooper is a full time lurker and part time amateur chronicler of internet behavior. She lives in New York City.
